About Me

I started my career in dentistry in 2007. While I trained as a dental nurse, I realised my real strength really wasn’t chairside — it was at the front of the business, supporting patients. Early on, I led oral hygiene assessment appointments for children and adults and taught tooth-brushing techniques. I quickly discovered that I was good at connecting with people and building trust, even in just a few minutes.

Later I spent most of my time explaining complex treatment plans for complete smile make overs and Orthodontic treatments, costs, and options in simple terms, and answered the questions people were often nervous to ask. My aim was always to help people feel calm, informed, and comfortable making a decision — without pressure or sales tactics. I loved being the person who bridged the gap between the clinical team and the patient, making sure nothing felt rushed, confusing, or overwhelming. In 2013, I joined SkinViva at a pivotal stage — before they had a bricks-and-mortar clinic. Over the next decade, I supported the business as it grew into one of the UK’s leading aesthetic providers and training academies.

During this time, I wore multiple hats contributing and offering support across all areas of the business — front desk, training support, enquiries, sales, and a little marketing. This gave me a deep understanding of how enquiries really work behind the scenes, and how easily good leads can be lost when a team is busy! One area I’ve always been particularly interested in is why people enquire in the first place — what’s going on in their mind, what they’re worried about, and what helps them feel ready to book. When that journey feels clear and human, it works better for everyone. After SkinViva was acquired by Harley Academy, I decided to step out on my own so I could support a small number of providers properly, rather than being spread too thin. Who I’m a Good Fit For We’ll work well together if: You’re getting enquiries but struggling to keep up with them You find it hard to follow up consistently You care deeply about client experience You don’t want hard sales or pressure tactics You want someone who understands your industry and your clients If you’re already seeing some success but feel things could run more smoothly behind the scenes, I can help. How I Can Help You It’s a great question — It’s a simple question, but an important one. Rather than telling you what I can do, I prefer to ask what would truly help you the most right now instead? What would make your day feel lighter? For some people, it’s not answering emails late at night. For others, perhaps it’s knowing that enquiries are being handled with care when you don’t have the time or headspace to engage in conversations during an already busy day. From there, we can connect the dots and build something that actually supports you. Here are some of the ways I can help: Look at how patients find you and suggest small improvements to make it easier for them to get in touch Review your website and the booking process to spot anything that might be stopping enquiries from turning into appointments Make sure every enquiry is replied to and followed up properly Answer calls, emails, and messages in a warm, professional way Set up automated processes, patient recalls , reminders etc so they continue silently in the background   Guide people from first contact through to booking Keep in touch with people who aren’t ready to book yet Manage diaries, follow-ups, and reminders Represent your brand in a way that feels genuine and consistent I can also help with lifestyle support such as booking travel, events, conferences, and personal appointments if needed. What I Don’t Do I always like to be super clear about what sits outside my realm of expertise. I’m always honest about my competencies, and if you request something that I feel would be better handled by someone else, I’ll let you know and can potentially refer you to a colleague.  I don’t create: Website or blog copy Social media content Health & safety documentation Policies and procedures, or HR documents CQC-related work Long applications (such as award submissions,) I’m also not a lead generator. My work begins once enquiries arrive. I help manage, engage, and convert the people who already reach out — and often help reconnect with people already in your database.
